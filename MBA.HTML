Master of Business Administration (MBA)

MBA or Master of Business Administration is a two-year full-time degree programme that deals with various general management and business practices based on real-world issues. An MBA degree is one of the most popular and sought-after higher management education options, especially for those who want to get into a managerial role.

Those who complete their MBA programme are some of the top earners in the management sector. Having a management degree also paves way for those who are interested in ising to the topmost position in the company. MBA admission in India is based on a student's academic and professional profile in addition to MBA entrance exams and GD-PI rounds.
