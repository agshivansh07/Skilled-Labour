Master of Science (M.Sc.)

M.Sc. stands for Master of Science and it is a two year postgraduate degree in Mathematics and scientific computing. Master of Science degree is designed to provide students with the required scientific knowledge and opens new and ample opportunities in a variety of fields.

If you have completed your Bachelor of Science and are looking for future options in the same field, you can explore the opportunities during M.Sc.
