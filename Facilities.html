Central Library

The Library is sanctum sanatorium in the temple of learning. It is the heart of teaching and research. The Central library has 1,22,670 Collection of books and bound periodicals.In order to facilitate all the readers in selecting the reading materials of their choice, the access to stacks is open to its members.The functionaries of Central Library are guided by the setup of LLRC and Working Group.

Health Center

The Institute Health Centre provides the following facilities to all its students and institute's employees and their dependents like OPD, Pathology, Physiotherapy, Dental, ENT, Psychiatry, Homeopathy, Ayurvedic

Banking and Post office

The campus has good banking facility with ATM for all the students and staff. Our campus houses following banks : Bank of Baroda (MNNIT Branch) and State Bank of India (MNNIT Branch) . A Post Office is situated at near to the Colony Gate.

Canteen

The Institute campus has excellent eating outlets, that serves food and cafeteria items at reasonable prices. An outlet managed by : Bikanervala Food Pvt. Ltd. and Amul India Pvt. Ltd.
